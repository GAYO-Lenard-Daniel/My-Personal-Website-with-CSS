About Me

As it is in the name, Underground Music are music that are relatively unpopular and typically goes against the grain of popular music. It usually implements more of a “Do It Yourself” ethos when it comes to the making of its music, which tends to create a more rougher, lo-fi sound in contrast to more clean and professionally produced music in pop music. Underground Music values sincere artistic and creative expression above all else and may disregard or even oppose traditional song structures and commercial appeal in order to reach such expressions. This leads to them becoming commercial failures with little mainstream attention due to their unconventional nature in their time, but would later on be rediscovered by a select few who recognize its unique quality and may grow entire cult followings around the artist and their music and may begin to receive acclaim from modern critics.
